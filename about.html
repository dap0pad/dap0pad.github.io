Our story

During the pandemic, plenty of people tried (and are still trying) new hobbies(opens in a new tab). For people who wanted to work with their hands, rug making was a natural choice, and before long, the rug-making scene exploded on Instagram and TikTok.

I've always been a fan of Instagram's DIY trends. I honestly don't know how many polymer clay jewelry(opens in a new tab) accounts I've followed after seeing them on my Explore page, but there are a lot. My Instagram Explore tab has recently been flooded with another form of craft: colourful tufted carpets.

So, out of nowhere, during the pandemic, I decided to try my hand at carpet tufting. Then I began sharing them on Instagram. My buddies then purchased rugs with their own unique designs from me. Following that, I understood that many individuals are willing to pay for this. I created a website and began marketing myself on tiktok and Instagram. And began to receive a large number of orders. I realised I couldn't do it alone, so I hired employees and started a business. We are now known as Costa Rugs and produce custom carpets.
